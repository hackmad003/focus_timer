# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 4, 2026**

## Introduction

Focus Timer Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Focus Timer Pro application (the "Service").

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

Focus Timer Pro operates entirely on your device. We do **NOT** collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data is stored locally in your browser using:

- **LocalStorage** - For application settings and preferences
- **IndexedDB** - For session history and statistics

### Data Types

The following data is stored **locally on your device only**:

1. **Timer Settings**
   - Focus duration preferences
   - Break duration preferences
   - Notification preferences
   - Theme preferences
   - Audio settings

2. **Session Data**
   - Session start and end times
   - Session types (focus/break)
   - Task labels (if provided)
   - Session completion status

3. **Statistics**
   - Total focus time
   - Number of completed sessions
   - Streak information
   - Daily statistics

## Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively to:
- Operate the timer functionality
- Display your statistics and progress
- Persist your preferences across sessions
- Provide you with notifications

## Data Storage

- All data is stored in your browser's local storage
- Data remains on your device and is never transmitted
- You have complete control over your data
- You can export your data at any time
- You can delete all data through the app settings

## Third-Party Services

Focus Timer Pro does **NOT** use:
- Analytics services (unless explicitly enabled by you)
- Advertising networks
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services
- Third-party tracking

### Optional Services

If you enable analytics (opt-in only):
- Anonymous usage statistics may be collected
- No personally identifiable information is shared
- You can disable this at any time

## Cookies

Focus Timer Pro does not use cookies. All preferences are stored in browser local storage.

## Data Security

- Data is protected by browser security mechanisms
- No data transmission to external servers
- All data remains under your control
- Use secure browsers and keep them updated

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data (view in app or export)
- Delete your data (reset statistics in settings)
- Export your data (JSON format)
- Control what data is stored

## Data Retention

- Data is retained until you clear your browser data or reset the app
- You can delete all data at any time
- Uninstalling the app removes all local data

## Children's Privacy

Focus Timer Pro does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

## Progressive Web App (PWA)

When installed as a PWA:
- The app works offline using cached resources
- No additional data is collected
- Service workers cache app files locally
- No data is sent to external servers
